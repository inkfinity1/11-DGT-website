Abouts:

Welcome to the world of Chinese cuisine. It is diverse and rich as the culture it represents. I chose to create a website about Chinese cuisine because I want to inspire the Western culture to understand and appreciate the many layers of Chinese cuisine. Many Westerners think Chinese cuisine is just rice, dumplings, and noodles, but it is so much more than that. By exploring the different types of culinary traditions, I hope this website inspires a deeper appreciation for the richness and complexity of Chinese cuisine, encouraging Westerners to embrace the full spectrum of flavours it has to offer.
