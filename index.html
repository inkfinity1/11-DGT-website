Chinese Cuisine

Chinese Cuisine is incredibly diverse and rich in history. This website explores the different regional cuisines of China, each with its own unique flavors, ingredients, and cooking techniques.

Chinese Cuisine comes from history that spans thousands of years, dating back to the Han Dynasty (206 BC-220 AD). It is believed that Chinese cuisine began to take shape during the Shang Dynasty (16th-11th century BC).

This website will dive into the eight major regional cuisines of China are Anhui (Anhui Province), Cantonese (Guangdong Province), Fujian (Fujian Province), Hunan (Hunan Province), Jiangsu (Jiangsu Province), Sichuan (Sichuan Province), Shandong (Shandong Province), and Zhejiang (Zhejiang Province).

The eight Chinese cuisines incorporate a variety of cooking methods, including stir-frying, steaming, deep-frying, and braising. These techniques have been honed over centuries to bring out the best flavors of seasonal ingredients.
